About Nutribite

Nutribite is an AI-driven health and nutrition platform that helps you make smarter dietary choices. It combines advanced AI models (Gemini), intelligent OCR scanning, and a data-driven diet planner to guide you toward a healthier lifestyle — all personalized just for you.

Key Features

AI Diet Planner

Generate personalized weekly meal plans using Indian foods. Supports calorie goals, allergens, and veg/non-veg preferences.

Image Calorie Detection

Upload a food photo and get its estimated calories and nutrition breakdown instantly using Gemini Vision + Indian Food Dataset.

OCR Ingredient Scanner

Scan food labels to extract ingredients, detect harmful additives like MSG or Maida, and view AI health analysis.

AI Recipe Generator

Get quick, oil-free, and calorie-counted healthy recipes generated by Gemini AI — customized for your ingredients.

BMI Tracker

Track your BMI, receive health category insights, and get AI recommendations for maintaining ideal weight goals.

User Dashboard

Personalized dashboard that saves your preferences, calories, and fitness data for a seamless experience every time.

Our Mission

Nutribite’s mission is to make healthy eating easy, intelligent, and enjoyable. We empower users with AI-powered insights and real-time data analysis — helping you eat right, live healthy, and feel great every day.
